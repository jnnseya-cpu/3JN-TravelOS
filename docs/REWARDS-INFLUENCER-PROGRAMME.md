## 2. Refer & Earn

- Unique referral link per user (existing `3JN-XXXX` code).
- **250 Travel ACUs** per successful **paid** booking by a referred friend. No cap on count.
- After the **20th** successful paid referral → unlock **Lifetime Revenue Share**:
  - **0.25%** of 3JN Travel's **net revenue** from every referred customer,
  - for as long as they keep booking through 3JN,
  - capped at **£20,000 per referred customer** (revenue share for that customer
    ends at the cap; all other referrals keep earning).

## 6. Commission Protection
- Revenue share applies **only to successfully completed & eligible** bookings.
- Commission = 3JN **net revenue after** refunds, chargebacks, taxes, supplier reversals.
- Revenue sharing ends at **£20,000 earned per individual customer**.
- Fraud, self-referrals, fake accounts, manipulated bookings → **immediate suspension
  and forfeiture** of rewards.
- Referral ownership: **last-valid attribution** within the approved attribution window.
- Accounts must stay **in good standing** to receive payouts.

## Profitability guard
Revenue share is a percentage of **3JN NET revenue** (our commission margin), never of
gross booking value — so a payout can never exceed what 3JN actually earned. The
£20,000/customer cap bounds lifetime liability per referred customer.
