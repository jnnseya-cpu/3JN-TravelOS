# 3JN Travel OS — Vendor Partner Programme

> Sell More. Earn Weekly. Grow Without Owning the Platform.

Approved individuals, retailers, travel agencies and travel agents sell products
and services inside the OS and earn commission **from the platform fee** —
without damaging platform profitability.

## 1. Core Commission Model
The platform charges a **10% service fee** on eligible sales. Commission is a
share of the SALE value, carved out of that fee:

| Tier | Platform fee | Vendor commission | Platform keeps |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Independent Vendor Partner** (not a registered travel professional) | 10% | **3%** | **7%** |
| **Registered Travel Agent / Agency / Retailer** | 10% | **4%** | **6%** |

Example (£1,000 ticket): fee £100 → independent vendor earns £30, platform keeps £70;
registered agent earns £40, platform keeps £60.

## 2. Monthly Top Seller Bonus
Each month's best-performing seller earns **+1%** for the **following month only**
(must be re-earned): independent 3%→4% (platform keeps 6%); registered 4%→5%
(platform keeps 5%).

## 3. Automatic Weekly Payments
All approved vendors/agents are paid **automatically every Friday**. A commission
is released only after: sale confirmed · payment cleared · no refund/chargeback/
fraud flag · booking validated · compliance checks passed.

## 4. Strict Registration & AI Risk Review
Access is not automatic. AI agents + compliance review screen every applicant:
identity verification · proof of address · online footprint · social credibility ·
fraud risk signals · previous business activity · reputation · behavioural risk ·
payment risk · travel-compliance risk · sanctions/blacklist screening · document
authenticity. Only passing applicants receive portal access.

## 5. Vendor Portal
Approved partners get a secure portal: sell eligible products · track sales ·
commission earned · customer enquiries · approved marketing materials · payout
status · performance ranking · compete for the monthly top-seller bonus.

## 6. Travel Professional Section
Registered professionals must evidence: company registration certificate · travel
agency licence (where applicable) · tax registration · business address · director/
owner ID · proof of professional activity · business bank account · compliance
declaration. Higher commission reflects stronger compliance, credibility and trust.

## 7. Profitability Protection Rules
- Platform ALWAYS keeps a minimum margin: independents 7% (6% in a bonus month);
  registered 6% (5% in a bonus month).
- No seller can exceed the approved rate unless authorised by management.
- **No commission on:** refunds · cancellations · chargebacks · fraudulent sales ·
  fake customer accounts · self-referrals · duplicate accounts · policy violations.

## 8. Positioning
Not a referral scheme — a full commercial partner network: trusted people,
retailers and professionals earn from every eligible sale they bring, while the
platform keeps control, protects margin, reduces fraud and builds a sales army
across markets. **Sell through the OS. Earn every week. Compete every month.**
